# Execution Roadmap: Phase 1 & 2

## Phase 1: The Foundation (Months 1-4)
*   **Core Engine:** Develop the "Asset Watcher" for local and cloud directories.
*   **Vision API Integration:** Implement multi-modal AI for image and video recognition.
*   **MVP UI:** Focus on the "Search & Discover" interface (Desktop & Web).
*   **Integration:** Initial plugins for Adobe Creative Cloud.

## Phase 2: Intelligence & Collaboration (Months 5-8)
*   **Custom Training:** Allow users to "teach" the AI their specific style or project-specific jargon.
*   **Team Libraries:** Collaborative tagging and shared asset pools for freelance collectives.
*   **Automatic Archiving:** Smart suggestions to move old projects to cold storage based on usage patterns.
*   **License Scanner:** Automated detection of stock asset metadata and expiration dates.

## Phase 3: Ecosystem Expansion (Months 9+)
*   **API for Developers:** Allow 3rd party creative tools to query the Asset AI.
*   **Marketplace Readiness:** Features for creators to package and sell their organized asset libraries.
